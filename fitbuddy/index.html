FitBuddy

To create something of my own has always been one of my goals. 

As soon as I moved to Berlin I had the opportunity to get in touch with a startup community and this environment really helped me to understand the power of creativity. No matter how many times one is going to fall, what really is important is not to give up on your idea.

Summertime is coming. The sun is shining. Parks are packed full of people.  This seems the perfect day to go outside and do some sport, yet I am not sure if any of my friends is available to workout with me. What could I do?

This brought me to design fitBuddy, a platform where you can meet gymaholics just like you who are willing to workout together for a day. 

At the moment, fitBuddy is only a personal project, a work in progress with a great amount of work still to do.

Starting from scratch with some sketches, I tried to figure out the main features of the app, what should I include? How can I keep things as simple as possible?

Registration will not be needed, clear path for the users with maximum 2 filters each steps, vivid icons and sections… these are going to be only some of the most important characteristics of the app.
